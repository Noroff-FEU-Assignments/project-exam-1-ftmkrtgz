CHILD DEVELOPMENT

Children are the most important elements that make up the future generations of people.


A child is a small human being in the process of human development and growth. Children differ in their age and level of development and are often engaged in activities such as learning, exploration and play. Children receive care and support from their families or other caregivers, and as they grow, they acquire more skills and knowledge.

Child Development

Child development is the growth and development process of people after they are born. Child development includes many physical, mental and behavioral changes, and these changes vary according to the age of the children, their families and their environment. Child development begins rapidly in infancy and this development process continues as the child grows. Child development enables the child to develop and grow physical, mental and behavioral skills, and this development process enables the child to acquire more knowledge and skills as he grows.

What are the main things to consider in child development?

  • It is important to show love and attention to the child. Children feel happier and safer when they receive love and attention, and this contributes to their development.
  • It is important to teach the child proper nutrition and a healthy lifestyle. Children are healthier and happier if they eat right and adopt a healthy lifestyle.
  • It is important to give the child an education appropriate to school age. Children can acquire more knowledge and skills by receiving an education appropriate for school age, and this contributes to their development.
  • Providing the child with a regular diet and good rest. Since children are in the process of growth and development, it is important to eat regularly and have a good sleep pattern.
  • To provide adequate physical activity opportunities for the child. Children enjoy movement and therefore it is important to provide adequate physical activity opportunities.
  • To support the mental development of the child. Children can support their mental development with games and fun activities.
